Ground Beef Stroganoff NoodlesRecipe

Description

These ground beef stroganoff noodles are a shortcut version of classic beef stroganoff in a one-pan version. The ultimate comfort food with flavorful ground beef, mushrooms, and egg noodles.

To make Ground Beef Stroganoff Noodles, begin by cooking 8 ounces of wide egg noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. While the noodles are cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.

Once the oil is hot, add 1 pound of lean ground beef and cook it until it's browned and no longer pink, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks. Stir in 1 diced onion and 2 cloves of minced garlic, cooking until the onion is softened and the garlic is fragrant, which takes about 2-3 minutes.

Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour over the beef mixture, stirring well to combine and cook for another minute. Next, pour in 2 cups of beef broth, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens slightly. Reduce the heat to low and stir in 1 cup of sour cream until it's fully incorporated and the sauce is smooth. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

Finally, add the cooked noodles to the skillet, tossing them gently to coat them evenly in the sauce. Allow the dish to simmer for a few minutes to heat through. Serve the Ground Beef Stroganoff Noodles hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy this comforting and flavorful meal!
